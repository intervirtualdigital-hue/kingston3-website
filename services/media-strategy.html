The problem with most media consultants

Most media consultants have never sat inside a newsroom, never built a relationship with an editor, never felt the pressure of a 5 p.m. deadline. They learn media from the outside, which means their advice is always one step behind the reality of how stories are actually assigned, written, and placed.

Fabian Lyon spent 17 years at the Miami Herald. He interviewed Kobe Bryant, Usain Bolt, and Nandi Mandela. He covered everything from high-stakes political moments to cultural milestones that defined South Florida's public identity. He knows what journalists are actually looking for — and more importantly, what makes them ignore a pitch entirely.

"There is no such thing as 'getting your story out there.' There is only getting your story to the right person, in the right frame, at the right moment. Everything else is noise."

That distinction is everything. And it is the foundation of every media strategy engagement at Kingston 3 Consulting.

What we actually do for you

Media Strategy at Kingston 3 is not a retainer where you pay monthly for vague "outreach." Every engagement begins with deep narrative architecture work — defining not just what you want to say, but the arc of how your public story develops over time.

We identify your core message, your target audiences, and the specific media ecosystems (national, trade, regional, digital) where your story belongs. Then we build a media strategy map: which journalists cover your beat, what angles will resonate, what timing will maximize impact, and how to position you as the source reporters come back to.

When you face a public challenge — a controversy, a rebranding moment, or a high-stakes announcement — Fabian brings crisis communications experience that is grounded in newsroom reality, not spin. We help you get ahead of the story before it gets ahead of you.

Who this service is for

This service is right for executives who want to own their public narrative, organizations navigating a pivotal moment, public figures looking to build sustained media presence, and anyone who has felt that their real story is being lost in translation somewhere between them and the public.

If you have something important to say and you have never been able to say it at the scale it deserves — that is exactly the problem we solve.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

How long does a media strategy engagement typically last?
Most engagements run 3–6 months at minimum, as sustainable media presence requires time to build momentum, relationships, and editorial placements. We also offer intensive project-based work for specific launches, events, or crisis situations.
Do you guarantee press coverage?
No ethical media consultant guarantees specific placements — journalists make their own editorial decisions. What we guarantee is that your pitch will be crafted with real newsroom expertise, targeted to the right reporters and outlets, and delivered at the right moment. Our placement rate reflects 17+ years of earned relationships.
Can you help with a crisis that is already in motion?
Yes. We take crisis communications engagements on an urgent basis. The first 24–48 hours of a public challenge are often the most critical, and Fabian's newsroom experience means he understands exactly how to respond with precision under pressure.
What types of media do you focus on?
Our focus is earned media — editorial coverage in print, digital, broadcast, and podcast formats. We work across national outlets, regional newspapers, trade publications, and digital platforms depending on where your target audience lives. We are not a paid media or advertising agency.
How do we get started?
Book a complimentary 30-minute discovery call via Calendly. Fabian will listen to your situation, ask the questions that matter, and give you an honest assessment of how Kingston 3 can help — and whether we're the right fit for your goals.
